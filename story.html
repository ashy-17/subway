Our Story

From a single sandwich shop to a global fresh food leader

Our Story

Subway is an American multinational fast food restaurant franchise that primarily sells submarine sandwiches (subs), salads, and drinks. It was founded by Fred DeLuca and financed by Peter Buck in 1965. The Subway story began in Bridgeport, Connecticut, when 17-year-old Fred DeLuca had a dream to become a medical doctor. To help pay for his education, a family friend suggested he open a submarine sandwich shop.

With a $1,000 loan from Peter Buck, they opened "Pete's Super Submarines" in Bridgeport, Connecticut. The goal was to open 32 stores in 10 years. Today, Subway is one of the fastest-growing franchises in the world with thousands of locations globally.

Our Mission

To provide the tools and knowledge to allow entrepreneurs to successfully compete in the Quick Service Restaurant industry worldwide, while maintaining our commitment to fresh, quality ingredients and outstanding customer service.

Our Values

  • Always serve fresh, quality ingredients
  • Provide outstanding customer service
  • Build strong relationships with customers and communities
  • Make a positive impact in every community we serve
